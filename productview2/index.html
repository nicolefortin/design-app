Custom A Frame
by Chiaozza

The series “A-Frames” explores color, shape, and display in the form of sculptural wall objects. Hand-cut using simple wooden joinery, their components have been slotted together and can be hung on a nail. They are ideal for displaying collections of small objects and curios, yet are beautiful on their own as art objects themselves.
